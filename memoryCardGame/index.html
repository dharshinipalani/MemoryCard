MEMORY CARD

INSTRUCTIONS:
Gameplay:
player start by flipping over any two cards in the grid. If the two cards match (same picture) then it will not flip back. If the two cards do not match, it will flip them back face down.
Remember:
Pay close attention to the cards that have been revealed and their positions on the grid. Try to memorize the locations of the cards to increase your chances of finding matching pairs.
Winning the Game:
The game continues until all the cards have been matched into pairs.
